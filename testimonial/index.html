Mentoring Philosophy

I have been involved with many educational systems and they all seem to have not changed much over hundreds of years. The world is changing rapidly and I believe that we need to develop a more personalized educational system that better meets the needs of different kinds of students and the challenges of our changing world. My activities have mostly involved working personally with students in accordance with my beliefs on the future of education. I intend to continue this work of personalizing our educational system, which benefits diversity, equity, and inclusion as well as academic performance for all students. The challenges facing humanity require that we utilize the unique contributions of all of the worlds citizens to create a better world.
